Questions

Conduit Terminal FAQ

What is Conduit Terminal?

Conduit Terminal is a desktop SSH client for Windows. It combines saved SSH hosts, host groups, split-pane terminals, SFTP, WSL shells, broadcast input, themes, jump host support, and encrypted credential storage.

Is Conduit Terminal free?

Conduit Terminal is free for personal use. Enterprise usage is $10/month, billed yearly at $120/year.

How do updates work?

The app checks the current release policy and shows a download banner when a newer Windows installer is available. It does not silently auto-install updates.

Why does the app register personal users?

Registration keeps the app ready for upgrades, license recovery, enterprise subscription checks, device tracking, and abuse prevention.
